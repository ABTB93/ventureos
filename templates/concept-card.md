## Interview Questions for This Concept Card

1. "What's your first reaction to this?"
2. "What resonates most with you? What feels off?"
3. "Does this solve a problem you actually have? How often?"
4. "How does this compare to what you do today?"
5. "Would you use this? Under what conditions?"
6. "Who else in your organization would care about this?"
7. "What would you expect to pay for something like this?"
8. "What would need to be true for you to recommend this to your team?"
