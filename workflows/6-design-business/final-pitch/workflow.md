## Overview

The final pitch is the culmination of the 12-week incubation. The venture team presents the full investor-ready pitch deck to the NVB and receives a formal Go / Pivot / Kill decision.

**This pitch must demonstrate:**
- Validated customer pain (FIP evidence, interviews)
- Defined ICP with buyer/user clarity
- Clear wedge + vision story
- Proven market opportunity (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Business model with revenue streams and unit economics
- Real traction signals (landing page, pilots, CAC, conversion data)
- GTM strategy and sales process
- 3-5 year financial projections
- Team, mothership advantage, and funding ask
